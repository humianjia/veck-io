Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information may be processed when you use Veck.io, how third-party services may interact with the site, and how to contact us with questions.

Information processed on the site

  • Basic technical data such as browser type, device type, approximate location, referring page, and page interactions may be collected through analytics tools.
  • If you email us directly, we receive the information you include in your message.
  • Embedded third-party game providers may process their own technical and usage data when you open a game frame.

Analytics and advertising

Veck.io uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site and may use Google advertising services where permitted. These services can use cookies or similar technologies to measure usage, improve performance, and support ad delivery.

Advertising disclosure: third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to this or other websites.

Cookies and local technologies

Cookies or similar technologies may be used for analytics, service performance, and advertising-related functions. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some third-party game or advertising behavior.

Third-party content

Some pages embed or link to third-party browser games. Those services operate under their own terms and privacy practices. Veck.io does not control the data handling practices of external game distributors or advertisers.

Children

Veck.io is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through direct contact with the site, please email privacy@veckio.space.

Your choices

  • You may stop using the site at any time.
  • You may disable cookies through browser controls.
  • You may contact us to ask policy questions or raise privacy concerns.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact privacy@veckio.space.
