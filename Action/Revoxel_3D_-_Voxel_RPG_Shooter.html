About Revoxel 3D - Voxel RPG Shooter

Revoxel 3D - Voxel RPG Shooter is a voxel-style action RPG shooter. Players explore a blocky world, complete quests, and battle enemies. The game features character customization, skill trees, and various loadout options and items.
